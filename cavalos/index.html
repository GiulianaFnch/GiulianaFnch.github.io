About Me

My background begins with working in riding schools in Brazil, where I assisted my trainer for about four years, helping to successfully produce young horses from different breeds, including warmbloods and Lusitanos.


In Portugal, I spent five months at a busy riding school living with about 25 horses, gaining valuable experience in different equine practices and environments.


In 2025 I spent three months working as a groom for a private dressage stables in Germany where I could gain a vast amount of rich insights about professional management of top sport horses.

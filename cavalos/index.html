About me

My background includes significant work in riding schools. In Brazil, I assisted my trainer for about four years, helping to successfully produce young horses from various breeds, including warmbloods and Lusitanos. In Portugal, I spent five months at a busy riding school living with about 25 horses, gaining valuable experience in different equine practices and environments.

Currently, I am seeking a more professional environment to further develop my skills. My dream in the future is to establish my own stud, where I can oversee horses from birth through their training, gaining knowledge of the entire process.

Specialities

I have experience starting young and green horses for show jumping and dressage, taking them to their first competitions and hackings. Comfortable working with stallions and difficult horses. Proficient in performing stable chores quickly and meticulously to high standards. Capable of working independently and rapidly learning new skills.

Type of Ride Work

I am experienced in riding a wide variety of horses, whether they are lazy, hot, green, young, or problematic. No type of horse is a challenge for me, and I am adept at learning how to deal with each individual horse. I appreciate the importance of flatwork and dressage, and I also love it. However, I am eager to expand my knowledge and skills in riding and dressage.
